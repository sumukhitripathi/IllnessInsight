Healthcare Reimagined

IntelliMed revolutionizes healthcare accessibility through AI-powered conversations, personalized coaching, and intelligent facility finding. Our platform serves seniors, children, anxious patients, and caregivers with empathetic, age-appropriate interactions.

  • Voice-enabled senior-friendly interface
  • Child-friendly avatars and storytelling
  • Empathetic AI with emotion detection
  • Proactive health coaching and reminders

Key Features

Comprehensive healthcare solutions powered by artificial intelligence

AI Chat Personas

Multiple specialized chat interfaces designed for seniors, children, anxious patients, and caregivers with empathetic responses.

Facility Finder

Interactive map-based search for hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and urgent care centers with real-time location services.

Health Coaching

Personalized goal setting, progress tracking, and AI-powered recommendations for fitness, nutrition, and wellness.

Symptom Checker

AI-powered disease prediction based on symptoms with urgency assessment and professional consultation recommendations.

Telemedicine

Virtual appointment scheduling, consultation booking, and emergency contact access for comprehensive healthcare support.

Voice Interface

Senior-friendly voice interactions with large fonts, simple language, and accessible design for all age groups.

Patient Journey

Comprehensive care from symptom assessment to post-visit follow-up

Initial Assessment

Patients begin with persona selection and basic health profile creation. Our AI adapts to their age, concerns, and communication preferences.

AI Consultation

Empathetic chat sessions with specialized personas. Seniors get voice support, children enjoy friendly avatars, and anxious patients receive gentle guidance.

Symptom Analysis

AI-powered symptom checker provides preliminary assessments, urgency levels, and recommendations for appropriate care levels.

Facility Matching

Interactive map helps locate nearby healthcare providers based on needs, insurance, and location preferences with detailed facility information.

Appointment Booking

Seamless telemedicine or in-person appointment scheduling with healthcare providers, including emergency contact options.

Post-Visit Care

Personalized health coaching, medication reminders, progress tracking, and follow-up care recommendations for ongoing wellness.
